# Test Case 1 - Register User

This document captures each step of Test Case 1 using the screenshots saved in this folder.

## Step 1
- Verify home page is visible successfully

![Step 1 - Home page visible](./step1_homepage.png)

## Step 2
- Click on 'Signup / Login' button

![Step 2 - Signup / Login](./step2_signup-login.png)

## Step 3
- Enter name and email address
- Click 'Signup' button

![Step 3 - Signup details entered](./step3_signup-filled.png)

## Step 4
- Verify that 'ENTER ACCOUNT INFORMATION' is visible

![Step 4 - Enter account information](./step4_enter-account-information.png)

- Fill details: Title, Name, Email, Password, Date of birth
- Select checkbox 'Sign up for our newsletter!'

- Click 'Create Account' button

![Step 5 - Account details filled](./step5_account-details-filled.png)

## Step 6
- Verify that 'ACCOUNT CREATED!' is visible

![Step 6 - Account created](./step6_account-created.png)

## Step 7
- Click 'Continue' button
- Verify that 'Logged in as username' is visible

![Step 7 - Logged in as username](./step7_logged-in.png)

## Step 8
- Click 'Delete Account' button
- Verify that 'ACCOUNT DELETED!' is visible and click 'Continue' button
